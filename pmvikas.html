PM Vikas Program

Pradhan Mantri Virasat Ka Samvardhan — a flagship initiative for the holistic development of minority communities through skill development, education, and livelihood support.

Empowering Communities Through Inclusive Development

The PM VIKAS program consolidates the key interventions of previously existing schemes, focusing on skill training, education, and economic empowerment of minority communities across India. It aims to provide holistic development through a multi-sectoral approach.

Key Components

Program Pillars

The PM Vikas Program is built on six core pillars that work together to create lasting, sustainable change.

01

Skill Development

Industry-oriented skill training programs for youth, enabling employment and entrepreneurship.

  • Market-driven
  • Certification
02

Leadership Training

Women's leadership programs fostering community leaders who can champion local development.

  • Empowerment
  • SHGs
03

Digital Literacy

Bridging the digital divide by equipping communities with essential technology skills and access to digital services, creating a pathway to the modern economy.

04

Livelihood Support

Financial assistance and market linkage programs helping artisans and micro-entrepreneurs grow their businesses sustainably.

  • Micro-enterprise
  • Market access
  • Crafts
05

Educational Support

Scholarships and coaching for students from minority communities.

06

Infrastructure

Community centers, hostels, and smart classrooms.
