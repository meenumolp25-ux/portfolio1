My Story

I am Meenumol Prakash, an engineering student at the College of Engineering Kidangoor and currently an IoT Assistant Intern for the PM Vikas Program at IIIT Kottayam. My journey began with a deep-rooted belief that education and technology should be used to uplift everyone.

Working as an IoT Assistant at IIIT Kottayam, I get to blend my technical skills with my passion for social empowerment. I help integrate modern technology solutions into the PM Vikas initiatives, ensuring that skill development and educational opportunities reach youth and marginalized groups more effectively.

Through grassroots engagement, awareness campaigns, and hands-on support, I have helped hundreds of families gain access to skill development programs, educational scholarships, and livelihood opportunities under the PM Vikas umbrella.
